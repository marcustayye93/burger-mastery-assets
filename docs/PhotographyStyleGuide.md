# Burger Mastery Photography Style Guide

All imagery must match the approved Burger Mastery batches.

## Visual requirements

- Portrait 9:16
- Photorealistic editorial food photography
- Dark charcoal stone background
- Warm directional studio lighting
- Natural colour and texture
- Premium, restrained composition
- Clean chef hands where required
- No jewellery or watches
- No text
- No logos
- No UI
- No watermarks
- No exaggerated saturation
- No cartoon or CGI appearance

Every image must teach one clear concept.
